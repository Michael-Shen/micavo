How can we help?

Contact support

For product questions, account help, billing issues, or privacy requests, email us at support@micavo.app.

Please include the email address connected to your Outshine account and a short description of the issue so we can help you faster.

Subscriptions and billing

Outshine subscriptions are managed through Apple. You can view, cancel, or change your subscription in your Apple ID settings.

  • Open the Settings app on your iPhone.
  • Tap your Apple ID.
  • Tap Subscriptions.
  • Select Outshine.

Account deletion

You can delete your account from inside the Outshine app. Deleting your account removes account data associated with your Outshine profile, but it does not cancel an active App Store subscription. Subscriptions must be managed through Apple.

Meeting audio and summaries

Outshine uses meeting audio to generate coaching, smart questions, and summaries. Meeting audio is processed for the product experience and may be removed after analysis as described in our Privacy Policy.
