The Eisenhower Matrix

Used by presidents, CEOs, and productivity experts worldwide. Distinguish between urgent and important tasks to work smarter, not harder.

🔥
Quadrant 1
Urgent & Important

Do First: Crises, deadlines, emergencies. Handle these immediately to prevent fires, but don't live here—it leads to burnout.

⭐
Quadrant 2
Important, Not Urgent

Schedule & Focus: Strategic planning, skill development, relationships. This is where success happens. Spend most of your time here.

📞
Quadrant 3
Urgent, Not Important

Delegate: Interruptions, some emails, other people's priorities. These feel urgent but don't contribute to your goals. Minimize or delegate.

📺
Quadrant 4
Not Urgent & Not Important

Eliminate: Time wasters, busywork, trivial tasks. These offer little value. Remove them from your list and reclaim your time.
